Derby beta phase

This years derby has reached its beta phase. And there’s still a ton of authors in the running. And likely a chunk of them that didn’t make it to beta phase, and still plan on publishing. Eagerly waiting for September 23rd to be able to see how many people made it. And hopefully having been able to publish my own derby book as well. Also waiting excitedly the end of october, when I will be able to reveal which one was mine. (Hopefully I’ve managed to finish it). I’m currently on a small vacation, although working on books and derby doesn’t stop for a vacation. But it is a good few weeks that I will be able to rest before school starts for me again. I’m hoping the summer has been going great for everyone. Happy reading to everyone. There’s some good books coming out in september for y’alls to-be-read lists.
