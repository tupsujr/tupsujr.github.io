Writing burnout

Coming back from my writing burnout and starting the actual work for my website has been intriguing. I’ve been very happy with the progress I’ve made so far even if it isn’t the best I can do. I’m currently primarily working on a book as a part of Inkfort publishing derby. Those who know what that is, are aware why I can’t tell any details about my book just yet. But I recommend checking it out here. I will do another post when the books publish, and I recommend checking them out. My secondary project, is my novel series set in the same world as my book The Hidden Valley, but 300 or so years later. It is only taking a backseat for the duration of the derby. Reading Hidden Valley before that is not necessary to enjoy the series. Lastly I’ve been doing work on my mailing list and website. It’s all been a lot of work, and I’m beyond grateful for all the help I’ve gotten.
