About me

A little bit about me

I am an aspiring author, with a focus on weird fiction and fantasy. I am 25 years old and have now published my second book, with plans for the 3rd one on the way! (latest next year's summer)

Right now my plans for the future would be to continue my studies while writing on the side, but hopefully I'll be able to make writing my main income someday. I am planning on at least finishing my "Starger gods than these" -series before starting a new one, but I will be joining the Derby competition, among others, every year! It will be quite a rough schedule, but I'm determined to keep it up :D

Books


The Hidden Valley

"Don’t pray to gods, they might answer."

Chili Hellyer is perfectly fine living among humans with her sister, working as a tailor. That all goes up in flame as a mysterious curse makes people snap and turn into blood thirsty monsters, and in the pandemonium the town ends up literally burning down. The desire to find out exactly what happened starts her on a journey back to the burned rubble – but with unforgiving desert and bored gods before her, she might never make it back at all...

This is my first book published as part of the Inkfort publishing derby 2022

Buy it!

Stranger Gods Than These

"Life was tipped on its head, when a normal night shift ends with Iris's best friend and co-worker being taken by something monstrous."

In a world where monsters just shouldn't exists, Iris finds the skeptical police no help. And when those monsters start appearing behind her door, she knows she needs to find out what happened and fast. But when it turns out that nothing is quite what it seems, and no one can be trusted, can Iris find the truth about her friends disappearance and save her.

This is my second book. It was also published as part of the Inkfort publishing Derby 2024
